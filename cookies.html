Last Updated: 20/1/2024
Welcome to SkillHub! This Cookies Policy explains how SkillHub uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By accessing or using SkillHub, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.


1. What are Cookies:

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit, preferences, and actions.


2. How We Use Cookies:

a. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of SkillHub. They enable you to navigate the site and use its features.

b. Performance Cookies: We use performance cookies to collect information about how visitors use SkillHub. This helps us improve the website's performance and user experience.

c. Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow SkillHub to remember your preferences and provide enhanced features, such as personalized content.

d. Analytics Cookies: SkillHub may use analytics tools that rely on cookies to gather information about user interactions on the site. This data helps us understand how users engage with our content.


3. Third-Party Cookies:

a. Third-Party Services: SkillHub may use third-party services that use cookies to provide additional features, analytics, or advertising on our website.

b. Social Media Cookies: We may integrate social media features that use cookies to track your interactions with social media platforms.


4. Your Cookie Choices:

a. Cookie Consent: By using SkillHub, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

b. Opting Out: You can opt-out of certain cookies by adjusting your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of SkillHub.


5. Changes to Cookies Policy:

SkillHub reserves the right to update this Cookies Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any updates.


6. Contact Us:

If you have any questions about our Cookies Policy, please contact us at [cookies@skillhub.com].
