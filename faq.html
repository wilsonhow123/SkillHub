FAQs

What is SkillHub?

SkillHub is a free and open-source education platform that thrives on a volunteer-based community. We provide accessible learning opportunities with a diverse range of high-quality courses contributed by educators globally.

Are the courses on SkillHub really free?

Yes, all courses on SkillHub are entirely free. We believe in making education accessible to everyone, and our commitment to open-source learning means that you can explore, learn, and grow without any cost barriers.

Is there a certification upon course completion?

Yes, SkillHub provides certificates of completion for certain courses. The availability of certificates depends on the specific course, and details can be found in the course description.

How to download your certificates?

Go to your profile and find the "Certificates" section. This is where you can view and manage all the certificates you have earned.

Can I access SkillHub courses offline?

Currently, SkillHub primarily operates as an online platform, requiring an internet connection for course access. However, we are exploring options to enhance offline accessibility for learners in the future.

How can I get support for technical issues or inquiries?

For technical support or general inquiries, please reach out to our support team via the contact form on our website. We're here to assist you in making your SkillHub experience smooth and enjoyable.

Are the courses on SkillHub suitable for all skill levels?

Yes, SkillHub offers courses catering to various skill levels, from beginners to advanced learners. You can filter courses based on your proficiency to find content that matches your current knowledge level.

How often are new courses added to SkillHub?

SkillHub regularly updates its course offerings. New courses are added based on community demand, volunteer contributions, and emerging trends in education. Check back frequently to discover the latest additions.

Are the courses on SkillHub self-paced?

Yes, the majority of courses on SkillHub are designed to be self-paced, allowing learners to progress through the content at their own speed.

How does SkillHub ensure the quality of its courses?

SkillHub maintains a rigorous review process for courses to ensure quality. Courses are often reviewed by subject matter experts and the SkillHub team before being made available to learners.

Where does the donation funds go towards?

Donations play a crucial role in sustaining and expanding SkillHub. They are used to maintain the platform, enhance features, and support initiatives that advance our mission of accessible education.

Are there any prerequisites for being a tutor on SkillHub?

While there are no strict prerequisites, a passion for education and a willingness to contribute to the learning community are essential. Apply to become a tutor with the form located in the home page.
